Link Pseudo-Classes Example with Distinct Background Colors

Click on the link below and observe the changes:

Click and hold to see active state

Explanation of each style:

  • Unvisited (a:link): Link is white text on a dark red background.
  • Visited (a:visited): After clicking, the link becomes white on a purple background.
  • Hover (a:hover): On hover, the link changes to black text on a light green background with an underline.
  • Active (a:active): While clicking and holding, the link changes to yellow text on a navy background.
